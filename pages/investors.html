Frequently Asked Questions for Investors
What types of investments does ANDE support?

ANDE's investor network participates in the full spectrum of SGB financing including equity investments, debt instruments, quasi-equity structures, and blended finance models. Member investors range from early-stage venture capital to growth capital funds to development finance institutions.

What is the typical investment size in ANDE's portfolio?

Investment sizes vary widely depending on the investor and SGB. ANDE members support investments ranging from $5,000 (microfinance) to $5M+ (growth capital). The network works with investors across all stages and sizes.

How does ANDE help with due diligence and risk assessment?

ANDE provides standardized due diligence frameworks, vetting tools, and access to market intelligence to support your investment decisions. Members also benefit from peer learning and lessons learned from 250+ organizations operating in emerging markets.

What is the Investment Manager Training Program?

Our training program equips investment teams with expertise in SGB investing fundamentals, impact measurement, portfolio management, and emerging market dynamics. Programs are delivered through in-person convenings and online modules.

How do I access investment opportunities through ANDE?

Members get access to ANDE's deal sourcing platform, investor networks, and partner referrals. Our team can help connect you with opportunities aligned with your investment thesis. You also directly access BDS providers, ecosystem leaders, and other investors in our network.

What support does ANDE provide for impact measurement?

ANDE provides standardized impact measurement frameworks, data collection tools, and access to outcome data from portfolio companies. We also facilitate peer learning on impact methodologies and impact-weighted returns analysis.

What are the membership costs for investors?

ANDE offers flexible membership tiers based on organization size and investment level. Please contact our membership team for detailed pricing and customized options for your organization.

How can my organization co-invest with other ANDE members?

ANDE facilitates co-investment through working groups, investor forums, and deal syndication networks. We connect partners with complementary investment theses and create structures for joint investments.
