Frequently Asked Questions for Researchers
What research topics does ANDE prioritize?

ANDE prioritizes research addressing evidence gaps in SGB development, including impact evaluation, business models, technology adoption, access to finance, gender equality, climate impact, and policy effectiveness. Review our research agenda for specific priority areas.

How much funding does the SGB Evidence Fund provide?

Grant amounts vary by project type and scope. Typical grants range from $50K for exploratory research to $500K+ for comprehensive impact evaluations. See the Evidence Fund website for current funding windows and opportunities.

Can we access data from ANDE partner organizations?

Yes. Researchers can request access to ANDE's impact measurement database, beneficiary surveys, and case studies subject to data sharing agreements and confidentiality protections. We facilitate access to support rigorous research.

How does ANDE support research translation to practice?

ANDE facilitates dissemination through publication platforms, convenings, policy briefs, and practitioner guides. We also help integrate research findings into training, capacity building, and strategic planning for ANDE members.

Can dissertations be funded through ANDE?

Yes. The Evidence Fund supports promising dissertation research aligned with ANDE's priorities. Graduate students and early-career researchers are encouraged to apply for support.

How do we engage with practitioners for field research?

ANDE facilitates researcher-practitioner partnerships. We connect researchers with willing ANDE members who can provide research sites, beneficiary access, and implementation support for collaborative studies.

What if our research focuses on a specific geography or sector?

ANDE's global network includes members across sectors and regions. We can connect you with practitioners in your geographic area or sector of interest to support research partnerships and data access.
