Frequently Asked Questions for BDS Providers
What types of BDS organizations can join ANDE?

ANDE welcomes all types of BDS providers including training organizations, consulting firms, chambers of commerce, industry associations, government agencies, and hybrid organizations. We have members of all sizes and organizational models.

How can ANDE help us connect with donors and investors?

ANDE facilitates partnerships between BDS providers and funders through member directories, networking events, and direct introductions. We also help showcase your organization and impact to potential partners and donors.

What training programs are available to members?

ANDE offers training on BDS fundamentals, emerging topics (digitalization, climate, finance), impact measurement, and client-facing skills. Training is delivered through convenings, online modules, and peer learning communities.

How do we participate in ANDE working groups?

Members are invited to join working groups based on their geographic focus and service areas. Working groups meet regularly for peer learning, knowledge sharing, and collaborative problem-solving.

Can we access ANDE's business tools and templates?

Yes. Members have access to ANDE's suite of business tools, training templates, impact measurement frameworks, and case study resources to strengthen service delivery and client impact.

What does ANDE membership cost?

ANDE offers flexible membership tiers based on organization size, annual budget, and geographic scope. Contact our membership team for pricing information tailored to your organization.

How does ANDE help with impact measurement?

ANDE provides impact measurement frameworks, data collection tools, and training to help you track client outcomes. We facilitate peer learning on measurement best practices and support ongoing improvement.

Can we collaborate with other ANDE BDS members?

Absolutely. ANDE facilitates collaborations between providers through working groups, referral networks, and partnership forums. Many members partner on larger initiatives and co-deliver services.
